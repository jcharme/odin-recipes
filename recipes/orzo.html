One Pot Creamy Garlic Orzo

Description

Quick and Easy Pasta Recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp margarine
  • 2 cups orzo
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup onion diced
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
  • 3/4 cup rice milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley
  • 2 tsp lime juice

Steps

  1. Melt 2 tbsp margarine in a large skillet over low-medium heat. Stir in onions, garlic & orzo and saut√© until orzo is lightly browned, about 5 minutes (stirring continuously & be careful not to burn the orzo).
  2. Slowly add the vegetable stock and bring to a boil. Lower to a simmer and cook for about 13 minutes, stirring occasionally (until the orzo is fully cooked).
  3. Once cooked, stir in the last 2 tbsp margarine, rice milk and nutritional yeast. While continuing to stir, bring back to a boil.
  4. Lower to a simmer and stir in salt, pepper, parsley & lime juice. Cook on low for another minute or so.
  5. Serve warm. Enjoy!
