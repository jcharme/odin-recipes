Mac & Cheeze

Description

This is a vegan mac and cheese recipe. It is not exactly like mac and cheese, but it is very rich and tasty sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 oz Rotini
  • 2 cups Steamed Broccoli Florets
  • 1 Cup Peeled, Finely Chopped Potatoes
  • 1/4 Cup Peeled, Finley Chopped Carrots
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1/3 Cup Raw Cashews
  • 1 Tbs Miso
  • 1 Tbs Tahini
  • 1 Tbs Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1/3 Cup Margarine
  • 1/3 Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • Black Pepper to taste
  • Paprika to taste

Steps

  1. Place the chopped potatoes and carrots in a sauce pan with a lid.
  2. Add 1 cup of water to the sauce pan.
  3. Boil covered until tender, 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add the cashews, miso, lemon juice, mustard, margarine, nutritional yeast, salt and pepper to the blender.
  5. Once potatoes and carrots are done cooking, add them and their cooking water to the blender.
  6. Blend until very smooth, add soymilk or water if needed.
  7. Cook the Pasta and Broccoli and mix together.
